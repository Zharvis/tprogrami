# Bento Box Home Page

**Status**: Accepted

We are introducing a Home page (`/`) to act as a unified landing hub for students, teachers, and admins. To organically integrate various modules (like the Schedule, and future modules like Tasks or Announcements) without relying on traditional disconnected cards or a strict chronological feed, we have decided to use a spatial "Bento Box" grid layout. 

This approach feels premium and modern, allows individual widgets to adopt unique shapes (such as a wide hero section for the Schedule), and establishes a scalable foundation for adding new modules seamlessly in the future.
